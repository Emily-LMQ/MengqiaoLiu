Kwai recruitment business payment function

Kwai's recruitment business provides companies and job seekers with a platform for mutual understanding and two-way selection. Company recruiters can display job positions in the Kwai live broadcast room and simultaneously broadcast the company's situation, job requirements and answer questions in real time. Applicants can submit resumes through the live broadcast room. If the company needs to recruit, it needs to pay for the platform. This involves recharge, usage amount, resume management, and other steps.

This project is to build the payment function of Kwai's recruitment business from scratch, and realize the construction of three platforms: mobile, web and software, as well as user processes from the perspectives of both recruiters and applicants.
